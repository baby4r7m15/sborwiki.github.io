One-Handed Weapons – The basic melee weapon type in Sword Blox Online: Rebirth. Ideal for fast attacks and mobility.
« Overview »

One-handed weapons are versatile and allow players to equip a shield or secondary item. They are great for PvE and PvP combat due to their speed and combo potential.

« Weapon Stats »
Weapon Damage Speed Special Ability
Iron Sword 12 Fast None
Steel Sword 18 Medium Stun
Obsidian Sword 25 Slow Bleed
« Tips & Tricks »
  • Use one-handed weapons with a shield for balanced offense and defense.
  • Perfect for combo attacks due to their quick swing speed.
  • Check special abilities – some can stagger enemies for PvP advantage.
