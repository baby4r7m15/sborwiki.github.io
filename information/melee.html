Melee Weapons
« Overview »

Melee weapons are one of the five weapon classes. Their Attack Speed is the quickest of the different weapon types, and their Attack stat is the lowest when compared to other weapon types of equal Skill Level rating.

  • Critical Chance of all Weapon Types is 15%, raising LUK up to max will add up to +5% Critical Chance, totaling 20% with 500 LUK invested.
  • Critical Hit damage is calculated as (base*crit multi) + (base*STR multi)
    • "STR Multi" ranges from 0 - 2 based on your STR points invested.
    • Critical Hits with Melee weapons will multiply your Base Attack stat by 3.
« Skills »

This weapon type currently has three unlockable sword skills. Reaver is a Universal sword skill that all weapons will unlock first at 5 Skill. Each new sword skill unlocked is stronger than the previous one, and capable of hitting more enemies at the same time. Investing points into AGI will increase your weapon's Attack Speed, the speed of the 4 lower level Sword Skills, and lower Sword Skill cooldowns.
